What the showroom is doing

Opening with a 120-person reception on Sept 8 evening — cocktails, a first look at the collection, photographable moments for the COS brand team. Then five days of by-appointment fittings (Sept 9–13), 20 guests max concurrent, with VIP client interviews and celebrity press throughout. The venue needs to hold both modes — a celebratory cocktail hour and a calm clienteling environment — without changing footprint.

What the venue needs to deliver

~6,000 sq ft of usable footprint, room types that read as architecture not event-rental, daylight for the day-long appointment program, and discreet enough that the reception can pivot to private fittings the next morning. Audio speakers for ambient music. 12 hours of access per day. Freight or passenger elevator to make load-in / out painless. Exclusive use across all seven days.

Included with every venue figure in this deck

On-site Studio Manager for every booked hour — vendor coordination, on-the-day problem-solving, deliveries, AV/music handling, the standard Cocoon service set you'd expect at any property. Plus a complimentary 30-minute site visit before booking, a 1-hour tech scout after booking, and 2 hours of Cocoon Concierge sourcing — caterer, DJ, bartender, photographer, florals — anything the COS team would rather we wrangle than wrangle themselves.
